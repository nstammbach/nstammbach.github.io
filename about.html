    Our Record Label

    Into Endless Chaos Records

    Into Endless Chaos is a Leipzig-based independent record label and management company that promotes the work of dark alternative artists since 1988. Into Endless Chaos has brought wave after wave of powerful, innovative, and often genre-defining music to the ever-hungry masses. It is this ethos that has seen Into Endless Chaos build up a stunning and diverse catalogue, weather the various storms facing any independent label, and in an age of declining record sales boast the most successful years of its existence as it strides into its fourth decade.
